Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Meat: This lasagna recipe needs a pound of ground meat (1/2 pound ground pork, 1/2 pound lean ground beef).
  • Onion: 1 diced onion to be mixed with groud meat and cooked until translucent.
  • Tomatoes: You need 2 diced tomatoes and 2 crushed tomatoes.
  • Fresh Herbs: One crushed garlic and two two tablespoons of crushed parsley to add flavor.
  • Sugar: To balance out the acidity from the tomatoes.
  • Spices and Seasonings: Dried basil, dried oregano, salt and black pepper.
  • Noodles: Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese: Cottage, Parmesan for layering and shredded mozarella for topping.
  • Cheese: Add creaminess to cheese and act as binding agent.
